# Privacy Policy — Where Am I? Last updated: 23 May 2026 --- ## Who we are Where Am I? is developed and operated by Philip Anderson, trading as an individual developer. Philip Anderson mail@philanderson.co.uk --- ## What data we collect When you use Where Am I?, we collect the following information. ### Account data - Your email address, used to create and identify your account ### Usage data - The names of places you create within the app - The date and time of each check-in and check-out session - The duration of time spent at each place ### We do not collect - Your GPS or device location — the app never accesses location services - Your real name, phone number, or payment information - Any data from your contacts, photos, or other apps - Crash or diagnostics data via any third-party SDK --- ## How we use your data We use your data solely to provide the app's features: - To authenticate you and keep your account secure - To store and display your check-in history and statistics - To sync your data across devices when you sign in We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below. --- ## Who we share data with **Supabase** — We use Supabase (supabase.com) to store your account and session data securely in the cloud. Supabase processes data on our behalf and is bound by data processing agreements. Their privacy policy is available at https://supabase.com/privacy. We do not share your data with any other third parties. --- ## Data storage and security Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and row-level security controls to ensure only you can access your own data. --- ## How long we keep your data We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request deletion of your data, we will remove all personal data associated with your account within 30 days. To request account or data deletion, email: mail@philanderson.co.uk --- ## Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR) If you are based in the United Kingdom or European Union, you have the right to: - **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you - **Correction** — ask us to correct inaccurate data - **Deletion** — ask us to delete your data - **Portability** — request your data in a machine-readable format - **Objection** — object to processing of your data To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mail@philanderson.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. --- ## App Store data declarations (Apple) | Data type | Collected | Use | Linked to identity | |---|---|---|---| | Email address | Yes | App functionality | Yes | | User ID | Yes | App functionality | Yes | | Usage data (check-in sessions) | Yes | App functionality | Yes | | Location data | No | — | — | | Crash & diagnostics data | No | — | — | | Contacts, photos, health, financial | No | — | — | --- ## Children Where Am I? is not directed at children under the age of 13.
